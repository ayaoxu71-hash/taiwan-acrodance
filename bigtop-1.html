🎪 專屬於特技馬戲的盛事與榮耀A Celebration of Acrobatics and Circus

2023 年為第一屆特技大鼎 BIG TOP 獎的創立之年。第一屆的備選已於 112 年 7 月 1 日至 7 月 30 日完成徵件，並確認由 32 名入圍進入各類審查觀察階段，頒獎典禮時間為 112 年 12 月 10 日 15:00，於國家衛武營藝術文化中心樹洞廣場辦理。

2023 marked the founding year of the BIG TOP Awards. Submissions for the first edition were collected from July 1 to July 30, 2023, with 32 nominees confirmed for the observation and review stage in each category. The award ceremony was held at 15:00 on December 10, 2023, at the Banyan Plaza of the Weiwuying National Kaohsiung Center for the Arts.

特技大鼎 BIG TOP 設置宗旨為：「三足鼎立，跨越特技、舞蹈、藝術，一藝孤行；獨門藝術。智仁勇，前瞻願景；深情執著；超越昇華」。希冀藉此獎項鼓勵臺灣特技人才，並期望能推動業界之間的鏈結與交流、進而提昇特技藝術內涵、擴充特技產業周邊效益、最終達成與國際交流鏈接的發揚為目的。

The BIG TOP Awards embody the spirit of the tripod cauldron: three legs standing firm across acrobatics, dance and art; a singular, dedicated craft; wisdom, benevolence and courage; a forward-looking vision; deep devotion; transcendence and sublimation. Through this award, the association hopes to encourage Taiwan's acrobatic talents, strengthen connections and exchange within the industry, elevate the artistry of acrobatics, expand the industry's wider benefits, and ultimately connect Taiwan with the international community.

入圍公告Nominees2023.09.01

✨ 32 名入圍者名單32 Nominees

由臺灣特技舞蹈協會主辦、衛武營國家藝術文化中心協辦的 BIG TOP 特技大鼎獎，於 9 月 1 日公布 32 名入圍者。本次入圍者名單針對各獎項指標核定，並考量以作品或個人分別評選，多位個人／團體同時入圍多獎項：

Organized by the Taiwan Acrobatic Dance Association with the Weiwuying National Kaohsiung Center for the Arts as co-organizer, the BIG TOP Awards announced 32 nominees on September 1. Nominees were assessed against the criteria of each category, evaluated by work or by individual, and several artists and companies were nominated in multiple categories:

  • FOCA 福爾摩沙馬戲團《苔痕》— 入圍 2 項，包括卓越創新獎及年度節目獎
  • Formosa Circus Art (FOCA), "Moss" — 2 nominations: Excellence in Innovation and Best Production
  • 蔡宏毅《行雲流劍》— 入圍 2 項，包括道具新創獎及技藝革新獎
  • Tsai Hung-Yi, "Flowing Clouds, Drifting Swords" — 2 nominations: Props Innovation and Skill Innovation
  • 創造焦點 — 以女馬系列完整版《#Since1994》及《保持聯繫 Keep in Touch》入圍卓越創新獎及道具新創獎
  • Eye Catching Circus — nominated for Excellence in Innovation and Props Innovation with the full Amazon Series "#Since1994" and "Keep in Touch"

入圍者評選標準皆以突破現有藝術框架、具未來發展潛力，並能為特技領域或技藝新創帶來嶄新演繹之能量的傑出個人或團體為主。Selection focused on outstanding individuals and groups who break through existing artistic frameworks, show strong future potential, and bring fresh interpretive energy to acrobatics and skill innovation.
