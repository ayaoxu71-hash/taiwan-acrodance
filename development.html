協會發展Development

六大核心發展方向，推動台灣特技舞蹈產業全面升級Six core pillars driving the comprehensive advancement of Taiwan's acrobatic dance industry

01
🏛️

協會發展Association Growth

持續壯大協會規模，擴大影響力與服務範疇，建立完善的會員制度與組織架構，推動特技舞蹈產業的整體發展。Continuously expanding the association's scale, influence and scope of services, building a sound membership system and organizational structure to drive the overall development of the acrobatic dance industry.

02
🎓

專業課程人才培育Professional Training

規劃系統性課程，培育下一代特技舞蹈專才。結合理論與實務，建立從基礎到進階的完整培訓體系，培養具備跨域能力的表演藝術人才。Designing systematic curricula to cultivate the next generation of acrobatic dance professionals — combining theory and practice in a complete training pathway from beginner to advanced, and nurturing cross-domain performing arts talents.

03
📜

專業證照檢定Certification System

建立國家級專業證照制度，提升產業標準。透過嚴謹的考核機制，確保從業人員具備專業資質，保障觀眾安全與藝術品質。Establishing a national professional certification system to raise industry standards — with rigorous assessment mechanisms that ensure practitioners are professionally qualified, safeguarding audience safety and artistic quality.

04
🌱

產業生態發展及需求Industry Ecosystem

研析產業需求，促進特技舞蹈生態系統健全發展。連結上下游產業鏈，推動演出、教育、技術等各面向均衡發展，打造完整產業生態圈。Analyzing industry needs and fostering a healthy acrobatic dance ecosystem — connecting the value chain and promoting balanced growth across performance, education and technique to build a complete industry ecosystem.

05
✈️

國際交流活動International Activities

舉辦及參與國際賽事、展演與文化交流活動。積極拓展海外合作網絡，讓台灣特技舞蹈藝術站上國際舞台，吸引世界目光，也引進國際視野。Hosting and participating in international competitions, performances and cultural exchanges — actively expanding overseas partnerships so Taiwan's acrobatic dance takes the world stage, attracting global attention while bringing in international perspectives.

06
🛡️

特技舞蹈職場安全與健康Workplace Safety & Health

推動職業安全教育，保障從業人員身心健康。建立安全標準作業程序，推廣運動醫學知識，確保特技舞蹈從業者在安全環境中工作與創作。Promoting occupational safety education to protect practitioners' physical and mental wellbeing — establishing standard safety procedures and sports medicine knowledge so acrobatic dance professionals can work and create in a safe environment.

特技大鼎 BIG TOP 獎項計畫BIG TOP Awards Program

近年臺灣推動特技產業連結以及展演，特技展演已然成為當代表演藝術新勢力。臺灣特技舞蹈協會為鼓勵特技人才，持續推進產業蓬勃發展，規劃特技獎項，支持特技人才持續創作能量、深入表演，進而推出專屬於特技人之獎項！

In recent years, Taiwan has been building connections and showcases for the acrobatics industry, and acrobatic performance has become a new force in contemporary performing arts. To encourage acrobatic talents and keep the industry thriving, the Taiwan Acrobatic Dance Association has created a dedicated award for acrobats — supporting their creative energy and deepening their artistry.
