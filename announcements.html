🎪 第二屆特技大鼎 BIG TOP 頒獎典禮 — 得獎名單2nd BIG TOP Awards Ceremony — Winners

臺灣特技舞蹈協會於衛武營國家藝術文化中心盛大舉行第二屆「特技大鼎BIG TOP 頒獎典禮」。典禮由衛武營總監簡文彬致詞揭開序幕。The Taiwan Acrobatic Dance Association held the 2nd BIG TOP Awards Ceremony at the Weiwuying National Kaohsiung Center for the Arts, opened by General & Artistic Director Chien Wen-Pin.

🏆 得獎名單Winners

  • 新星獎　張少齊〈Fly〉— 以扯鈴為核心，融入Acrodance、街舞節奏與馬戲翻騰。榮獲2025年法國「明日馬戲藝術節」銀獎與觀眾票選獎。
  • Rising Star Award  Zhang Shao-Qi, "Fly" — diabolo-centered work fusing Acrodance, street-dance rhythm and circus tumbling; Silver Medal and Audience Award winner at the 2025 Festival Mondial du Cirque de Demain in France.
  • 年度節目金獎　FOCASA《幾米男孩的100次勇敢》— 與林懷民首度合作，打造沉浸式帳篷馬戲樂園，27場全數售罄。
  • Gold Award for Best Production  FOCASA, "The Boy Who Was Brave 100 Times" — first collaboration with Lin Hwai-Min; an immersive tent-circus wonderland with all 27 performances sold out.
  • 年度節目銀獎　阮劇團《鬼地方》— 改編金典獎小說，以念歌、電音、特技、雜耍與弄鐃馬戲交織呈現。
  • Silver Award for Best Production  Our Theatre, "Ghost Town" — adapted from the award-winning novel, weaving chant, electronic music, acrobatics, juggling and nao-cymbal circus.
  • 道具新創獎　即將成真火舞團《日出昇龍》— 突破傳統形式，將火焰融入東方技藝與動作語彙。
  • Props Innovation Award  Coming True Fire Dance Troupe, "Sunrise, Rising Dragon" — breaking traditional forms by fusing flame with Eastern skills and movement.
  • 個人風雲獎　陳冠廷 — 從專業表演者進化為兼具編導能力的創作者，《嘛係人》等作品巡演國際。
  • Best Personal Award  Chen Guan-Ting — performer turned director-creator whose works, including "Mā Sī Lâng (Also Human)," tour internationally.
  • 技藝革新獎　吳顥中《扯鈴的畫》— 將扯鈴從技術框架抽離，轉化為行動藝術與視覺創作。
  • Skill Innovation Award  Wu Hao-Zhong, "Diabolo Paintings" — freeing the diabolo from its technical frame into performance art and visual creation.
  • 卓越創新獎　FOCASA《馬戲藝術節》— 善用城市空間與帳篷，融合展演、策展與教育推廣。
  • Excellence in Innovation Award  FOCASA Circus Festival — inventive use of urban space and tents, blending performance, curation and education.
  • 最具影響力獎　研華文教基金會 — 長期深耕藝術教育與人才培育，支持特技與馬戲發展。
  • Most Influential Award  Advantech Foundation — long-term dedication to arts education and talent development in support of acrobatics and circus.
第二屆入圍2nd Ed. Nominees2025.08.25

🎪 第二屆 BIG TOP 特技大鼎獎 入圍名單（22組）2nd BIG TOP Awards — 22 Nominees

由臺灣特技舞蹈協會主辦，衛武營國家藝術文化中心合辦，正式公布22位/組入圍名單。評審依各獎項指標，綜合考量作品完整度與個人表現。Organized by the Taiwan Acrobatic Dance Association with the Weiwuying National Kaohsiung Center for the Arts, the list of 22 nominees was officially announced, judged on each category's criteria, work completeness and individual performance.

✨ 本屆亮點Highlights

  • 即將成真火舞團《日出昇龍》勇奪「道具新創獎」、「技藝革新獎」、「年度節目獎」三項入圍
  • Coming True Fire Dance Troupe, "Sunrise, Rising Dragon" — three nominations: Props Innovation, Skill Innovation and Best Production
  • 阮劇團《鬼地方》同時入圍「卓越創新獎」、「年度節目獎」
  • Our Theatre, "Ghost Town" — nominated for Excellence in Innovation and Best Production
  • FOCASA馬戲團憑《解憂快餐車》、《幾米男孩的100次勇敢》、《馬戲藝術節》入圍多項
  • FOCASA Circus — multiple nominations for "The Comfort Food Truck," "The Boy Who Was Brave 100 Times" and the Circus Festival
  • 葉時廷新創雜技《拋接遊戲機器》同時入圍「道具新創獎」、「個人風雲獎」
  • Yeh Shih-Ting, "Juggling Game Machine" — nominated for Props Innovation and Best Performer
  • 陳萱丞《雙星太極》入圍「技藝革新獎」、「個人風雲獎」
  • Chen Xuan-Cheng, "Twins Wheel" — nominated for Skill Innovation and Best Performer
  • 張少齊 Fly《Sublime》入圍「新星獎」、「技藝革新獎」
  • Zhang Shao-Qi (Fly), "Sublime" — nominated for Rising Star and Skill Innovation
