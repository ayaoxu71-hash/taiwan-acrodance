計畫說明About the Program

為促進兩岸特技與雜技藝術交流，提供臺灣青年表演者向專業團隊學習的機會，特舉辦本專業訓練計畫。歡迎對特技、雜技、馬戲表演具備基礎與熱忱的青年踴躍報名，透過系統性的專業訓練，精進個人技藝、拓展國際視野。

This professional training program was created to promote cross-strait exchange in acrobatics and to give young Taiwanese performers the opportunity to learn from a professional company. Young performers with a foundation in — and passion for — acrobatics, juggling and circus are warmly encouraged to apply, refining their skills and broadening their horizons through systematic professional training.

報名與詳細徵選辦法，請洽臺灣特技舞蹈協會，或來信 taiwanacrodance.ep@gmail.com 洽詢。

For application details and selection guidelines, please contact the Taiwan Acrobatic Dance Association at taiwanacrodance.ep@gmail.com.
