講座簡介About the Symposium

探索當代馬戲創生，打破對馬戲的傳統印象。邀請來自不同國家的國際馬戲策展與製作人，從不同視角分享實務與創作歷程，開拓嶄新的馬戲視野。

Exploring contemporary circus creation and challenging traditional notions of the art form, this symposium brings together international circus curators and producers from around the world to share their practices and creative journeys from different perspectives — opening fresh horizons for circus.
