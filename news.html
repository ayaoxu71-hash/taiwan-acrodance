活動Event2025.12.07

傳承特技藝術！第二屆「特技大鼎 BIG TOP 頒獎典禮」得獎名單出爐2nd BIG TOP Acrobatic Awards Ceremony — Winners Announced

臺灣特技舞蹈協會於衛武營國家藝術文化中心盛大舉行第二屆「特技大鼎 BIG TOP 頒獎典禮」，典禮由衛武營總監簡文彬致詞揭開序幕。本屆頒出 8 大獎項，包含新星獎（張少齊）、年度節目金獎（FOCASA《幾米男孩的 100 次勇敢》）等。The Taiwan Acrobatic Dance Association held the 2nd BIG TOP Awards Ceremony at the Weiwuying National Kaohsiung Center for the Arts, opened by General & Artistic Director Chien Wen-Pin. Eight awards were presented, including the Rising Star Award (Zhang Shao-Qi) and the Gold Award for Best Production (FOCASA's "The Boy Who Was Brave 100 Times").

活動Event2025.12.06

【2025 衛武營馬戲平台】國際講座2025 Weiwuying Circus Platform — International Symposium

探索當代馬戲生態，打破對馬戲的傳統印象。邀請來自捷克、柬埔寨、澳洲、加拿大、法國及台灣的國際馬戲策展與製作人，從不同視角分享實務與創作歷程。
時間：2025/12/6 (Sat) 11:00–13:00
地點：衛武營國家藝術文化中心 演講廳Exploring the contemporary circus ecosystem and challenging traditional notions of circus. International circus curators and producers from the Czech Republic, Cambodia, Australia, Canada, France and Taiwan share their practices and creative journeys.
Time: Sat, Dec 6, 2025, 11:00–13:00
Venue: Lecture Hall, Weiwuying National Kaohsiung Center for the Arts

紀事Record2022.08.28

111 年 8 月 28 日第一屆會員大會1st General Member Assembly (August 28, 2022)

依內政部社會團體法組織規範，經本協會籌備委員會草擬，提交會員大會審議通過後，報主管機關核備。本協會入會費用以一次為原則，常年會費每年繳交一次，各位會員切勿任意斷會。In accordance with the Ministry of the Interior's regulations on social organizations, documents drafted by the preparatory committee were reviewed and adopted by the General Member Assembly and filed with the competent authority. The admission fee is a one-time payment and the annual fee is paid yearly; members are asked to keep their membership active.
