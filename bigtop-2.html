🎪 傳承特技藝術・迎向國際舞台Honoring the Art of Acrobatics, Embracing the World Stage

臺灣特技舞蹈協會於 12 月 7 日，假衛武營國家藝術文化中心盛大舉行第二屆「特技大鼎 BIG TOP 頒獎典禮」，現場嘉賓雲集、氣氛隆重，充分展現臺灣特技與馬戲藝術蓬勃發展的能量。集結臺灣當代特技、馬戲與跨界創作的高光時刻，這不只是一場頒獎典禮，更是臺灣特技藝術邁向永續與世界的重要里程碑。

On December 7, the Taiwan Acrobatic Dance Association held the 2nd BIG TOP Awards Ceremony at the Weiwuying National Kaohsiung Center for the Arts. With a distinguished audience and a festive atmosphere, the event showcased the flourishing energy of Taiwan's acrobatics and circus arts. Gathering the highlights of Taiwan's contemporary acrobatics, circus and cross-disciplinary creation, it was more than an award ceremony — it marked a milestone on Taiwan's path toward a sustainable, world-facing acrobatic art.

典禮由衛武營國家藝術文化中心總監簡文彬致詞揭開序幕。簡文彬總監表示，衛武營與臺灣特技舞蹈協會多年來持續合作，已不僅止於藝術創作與節目交流，更透過本頒獎典禮，實質支持並激發特技領域人才，為臺灣創新特技藝術的永續發展奠定關鍵基礎。

The ceremony opened with remarks by Chien Wen-Pin, General and Artistic Director of Weiwuying. He noted that the long-standing collaboration between Weiwuying and the association goes beyond artistic creation and program exchange: through this ceremony, it gives concrete support and inspiration to acrobatic talents, laying a crucial foundation for the sustainable development of Taiwan's innovative acrobatic arts.

衛武營馬戲平台策展人耿一偉亦指出，近年來臺灣當代馬戲、特技與雜耍的創作能量日益成熟，逐漸形塑出獨特樣貌；未來如何跳脫既有框架、創作具深度且具國際競爭力的作品，是整體產業最值得期待的發展方向。

Keng Yi-Wei, curator of the Weiwuying Circus Platform, added that the creative energy of Taiwan's contemporary circus, acrobatics and juggling has matured in recent years, gradually taking on a distinctive character. How to break free of existing frameworks and create works of depth and international competitiveness is the most promising direction for the industry.

🏆 象徵精神與榮耀的獎座設計A Trophy of Spirit and Honor

本屆「特技大鼎」獎座由蔡爾平大師親自操刀，精心客製打造，以「鼎」為核心意象，象徵臺灣特技馬戲堅韌不拔的精神底蘊，以及藝術不斷昇華、代代傳承的理想。

This year's BIG TOP trophy was custom-crafted by master artist Tsai Er-Ping. Centered on the image of the "Ding" — the ancient tripod cauldron — it symbolizes the resilient spirit of Taiwan's acrobatics and circus, and the ideal of an art that keeps ascending, passed on from generation to generation.

🌟 八大獎項・得獎名單Winners of the Eight Awards

本屆典禮邀集多位表演藝術領域重量級專業評審，從眾多優秀創作中，遴選出 22 位（組）入圍決選者，最終得獎名單如下：

A jury of leading professionals from the performing arts selected 22 finalists from a wealth of outstanding works. The winners are:

  • 新星獎　Rising Star Award
    張少齊〈Fly〉— 以扯鈴為創作核心，融合 Acrodance、街舞節奏與馬戲翻騰語彙，為當代扯鈴開創全新表現典範。作品於 2025 年法國「明日馬戲藝術節」榮獲銀獎與觀眾票選獎，展現高度國際潛力。
  • Rising Star Award
    Zhang Shao-Qi, "Fly" — Built around the diabolo and fusing Acrodance, street-dance rhythms and circus tumbling, the work sets a new paradigm for contemporary diabolo performance. It won the Silver Medal and the Audience Award at the 2025 Festival Mondial du Cirque de Demain in France, showing remarkable international potential.
  • 年度節目金獎　Gold Award for Best Production
    FOCASA《幾米男孩的 100 次勇敢》— FOCASA 成團 15 週年之際，首度與雲門舞集創辦人林懷民合作，結合幾米知名 IP，於台南水交社園區打造沉浸式帳篷馬戲樂園。27 場演出全數售罄，高雄加演亦迅速完售，成為 2025 年臺灣表演藝術界的重要指標盛事。
  • Gold Award for Best Production
    FOCASA, "The Boy Who Was Brave 100 Times" — For its 15th anniversary, FOCASA collaborated for the first time with Cloud Gate founder Lin Hwai-Min, bringing illustrator Jimmy Liao's beloved IP to life as an immersive tent-circus wonderland at Tainan's Shuijiaoshe Park. All 27 performances sold out, as did the added Kaohsiung run — a landmark event of Taiwan's performing arts in 2025.
  • 年度節目銀獎　Silver Award for Best Production
    阮劇團《鬼地方》— 改編自金典獎小說，融合念歌、電音、特技、雜耍與弄鐃馬戲，交織出魔幻寫實的鄉野風景與土地記憶，展現劇團跨界創作的代表成果。
  • Silver Award for Best Production
    Our Theatre, "Ghost Town" — Adapted from the Taiwan Literature Award-winning novel, it weaves liām-kua chant, electronic music, acrobatics, juggling and nao-cymbal circus into a magical-realist landscape of rural memory — a signature achievement of the company's cross-disciplinary work.
  • 道具新創獎　Props Innovation Award
    即將成真火舞團《日出昇龍》— 突破傳統火舞形式，將火焰與東方技藝與動作語彙結合，打造能量奔放、視覺震撼的舞台語言，拓展火舞與馬戲的美學邊界。
  • Props Innovation Award
    Coming True Fire Dance Troupe, "Sunrise, Rising Dragon" — Breaking with traditional fire-dance forms, it fuses flame with Eastern skills and movement vocabulary, creating an exuberant, visually stunning stage language that expands the aesthetic boundaries of fire dance and circus.
  • 個人風雲獎　Best Personal Award
    陳冠廷 — 從專業表演者蛻變為兼具編導能力的創作者，《嘛係人》、《解憂快餐車》等作品巡演國際、廣受好評，是推動臺灣馬戲走向世界的重要推手。
  • Best Personal Award
    Chen Guan-Ting — Evolving from professional performer into a creator with directing and choreographic command, his works such as "Mā Sī Lâng (Also Human)" and "The Comfort Food Truck" have toured internationally to acclaim, making him a key force in bringing Taiwanese circus to the world.
  • 技藝革新獎　Skill Innovation Award
    吳顥中《扯鈴的畫》— 以「轉譯」為創作核心，將扯鈴從技術展示中解放，轉化為行動藝術與視覺創作，並邀請觀眾共同參與，展現技藝革新與藝術實驗的跨界可能。
  • Skill Innovation Award
    Wu Hao-Zhong, "Diabolo Paintings" — With "translation" at its core, it frees the diabolo from technical display, transforming it into performance art and visual creation with audience participation — opening cross-disciplinary possibilities between skill innovation and artistic experiment.
  • 卓越創新獎　Excellence in Innovation Award
    FOCASA《馬戲藝術節》— 善用城市空間、園區與帳篷，融合展演、策展與教育推廣，打造嘉年華式體驗，建立臺灣少見的移動式馬戲節慶典範。
  • Excellence in Innovation Award
    FOCASA Circus Festival — Making inventive use of urban spaces, parks and tents, it blends performance, curation and education into a carnival-like experience, establishing a rare model of a traveling circus festival in Taiwan.
  • 最具影響力獎　Most Influential Award
    研華文教基金會 — 長期深耕藝術教育與人才培育，以穩健策略支持特技與馬戲產業發展，猶如支撐產業的三角基座，是推動臺灣馬戲生態前進的重要關鍵力量。
  • Most Influential Award
    Advantech Foundation — With long-term dedication to arts education and talent development, it steadily supports the acrobatics and circus industry — like the tripod's base that holds up the whole, a key force driving Taiwan's circus ecosystem forward.

💬 評審委員評語Jury Comments

  • 即將成真火舞團《日出昇龍》
    大膽將火焰元素熔鑄於傳統特技框架中。作品不僅在視覺上創造了震撼的感官詩篇，更以火為魂，為經典技藝注入熾熱的生命力，成功昇華了馬戲的藝術維度，開創出屬於臺灣、獨具東方底蘊的磅礡技藝美感。
  • Coming True Fire Dance Troupe, "Sunrise, Rising Dragon"
    Boldly forging fire into the framework of traditional acrobatics, the work creates a stunning sensory poem — with fire as its soul, it breathes blazing life into classic skills, elevates the artistic dimension of circus, and establishes a majestic aesthetic of technique that is distinctly Taiwanese and deeply Eastern.
  • 張少齊〈Fly〉
    為扯鈴藝術開啟了嶄新起點。他打破框架，將 Acrodance 的流動、街舞的節奏與馬戲翻騰融於一爐，建構出獨樹一格的表演形態。流暢的編創成功跨越文化界線，更榮獲 2025 年第 44 屆「法國明日馬戲藝術節」銀獎與觀眾票選獎。
  • Zhang Shao-Qi, "Fly"
    A new starting point for the art of diabolo. Breaking conventions, he melds the flow of Acrodance, the rhythm of street dance and circus tumbling into a singular performance style. His fluent creation crosses cultural boundaries, earning the Silver Medal and Audience Award at the 44th Festival Mondial du Cirque de Demain in 2025.
  • 吳顥中《扯鈴的畫》
    展現極高的成熟度與原創性。以轉譯的概念出發，將「物件」從原有的技術語境中抽離，轉化為「畫作」的行動藝術，讓技藝與視覺創作之間產生對話。他能與觀眾共同參與創作過程，呈現出臺灣馬戲在技藝革新與藝術實驗之間的新可能。
  • Wu Hao-Zhong, "Diabolo Paintings"
    Remarkably mature and original. Starting from the concept of translation, he lifts the "object" out of its technical context and turns it into the performance art of "painting," creating a dialogue between skill and visual creation. By inviting the audience into the creative process, he reveals new possibilities for Taiwanese circus between skill innovation and artistic experiment.
  • 研華文教基金會
    長期深耕藝術教育領域，在這個領域尚未被廣泛關注之前，便已著手推動人才培育與教育支持，展現前瞻視野與堅定承諾。從學校教育的根本著手，協助建立可長可久的培養體系，正如一個支撐塔頂的三角基座，穩固而關鍵。
  • Advantech Foundation
    Long devoted to arts education, the foundation began fostering talent and supporting education before the field drew wide attention — showing foresight and firm commitment. Working from the roots of school education, it helps build a lasting training system: like the tripod base beneath the tower's peak, steady and essential.

✨ 入圍名單（22 組）本屆亮點Highlights from the 22 Nominees

由臺灣特技舞蹈協會主辦，衛武營國家藝術文化中心合辦，於 8/25 正式公布 22 位／組入圍名單。評審依各獎項指標，綜合考量作品完整度與個人表現，選出能突破藝術框架、展現未來潛力的傑出個人與團隊。

Organized by the Taiwan Acrobatic Dance Association with the Weiwuying National Kaohsiung Center for the Arts, the list of 22 nominees was announced on August 25. Judging against each category's criteria — weighing the completeness of the work and individual performance — the jury selected outstanding artists and companies who break artistic frameworks and show future potential.

  • 即將成真火舞團《日出昇龍》— 勇奪「道具新創獎」、「技藝革新獎」、「年度節目獎」三項入圍
  • Coming True Fire Dance Troupe, "Sunrise, Rising Dragon" — three nominations: Props Innovation, Skill Innovation and Best Production
  • 阮劇團《鬼地方》— 同時入圍「卓越創新獎」、「年度節目獎」
  • Our Theatre, "Ghost Town" — nominated for both Excellence in Innovation and Best Production
  • FOCASA 馬戲團 — 憑《解憂快餐車》、《幾米男孩的 100 次勇敢》、《馬戲藝術節》入圍多項
  • FOCASA Circus — multiple nominations for "The Comfort Food Truck," "The Boy Who Was Brave 100 Times" and the Circus Festival
  • 狂夢藝術《美好的事》— 馬戲移動式劇場，入圍年度節目獎
  • Kuang Meng Art, "Beautiful Things" — a traveling circus theatre piece, nominated for Best Production
  • 葉時廷新創雜技《拋接遊戲機器》— 同時入圍「道具新創獎」、「個人風雲獎」
  • Yeh Shih-Ting, new juggling work "Juggling Game Machine" — nominated for Props Innovation and Best Performer
  • 陳萱丞《雙星太極 – Twins Wheel》— 入圍「技藝革新獎」、「個人風雲獎」
  • Chen Xuan-Cheng, "Twins Wheel" — nominated for Skill Innovation and Best Performer
  • 張少齊 Fly《Sublime》— 入圍「新星獎」、「技藝革新獎」
  • Zhang Shao-Qi (Fly), "Sublime" — nominated for Rising Star and Skill Innovation

BIG TOP 的核心精神：「三足鼎立，跨越特技、舞蹈、藝術；一藝孤行，獨門藝術。智仁勇，前瞻願景；深情執著，超越昇華。」The core spirit of BIG TOP: three legs standing firm across acrobatics, dance and art; a singular, dedicated craft. Wisdom, benevolence and courage; a forward-looking vision; deep devotion; transcendence and sublimation.

🤝 新生代能量・共築永續未來New-Generation Energy, a Sustainable Future Together

典禮特別邀請新生代馬戲表演者廖恩沛與曹宥廉現場呈現個人創作，專業且極具張力的演出，充分展現新世代特技馬戲的活力與創意。

The ceremony featured live performances of original works by new-generation circus artists Liao En-Pei and Cao You-Lian — professional, gripping performances that showcased the vitality and creativity of the next generation of acrobatics and circus.

臺灣特技舞蹈協會榮譽理事長林佳鋒與理事長陳俊安代表全體會員，向所有得獎者致上最誠摯的祝賀，並感謝所有入圍者、專業評審及蒞臨現場的貴賓。正是大家的共同投入與支持，讓臺灣特技與馬戲藝術持續拓展視野、累積高度，邁向永續經營的未來。

On behalf of all members, Honorary President Lin Jia-Feng and President Chen Jun-An extended their warmest congratulations to the winners, and thanked all nominees, jurors and guests. It is this shared dedication and support that keeps Taiwan's acrobatics and circus arts broadening their horizons, reaching new heights, and moving toward a sustainable future.
