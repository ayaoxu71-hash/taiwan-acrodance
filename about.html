簡介About the Association

臺灣特技舞蹈協會成立於臺灣臺北，111年由陳碧涵、林佳鋒、李曉蕾等34人發起後組織籌備委員會，其中由陳碧涵擔任主任委員督導籌備期間工作。同年7月公開招募會員並於8月28日辦理第一屆會員大會、9月4日召開第一屆第一次理監事聯席會議。在經由兩次會議投票結果後，最終由林佳鋒當選本會第一屆理事長主導本會未來發展。

The Taiwan Acrobatic Dance Association (TADA) was established in Taipei, Taiwan. In 2022, 34 founding members — including Chen Bi-Han, Lin Jia-Feng and Li Xiao-Lei — initiated the association and formed a preparatory committee, chaired by Chen Bi-Han, to oversee its founding. Members were publicly recruited in July of the same year; the first General Member Assembly was held on August 28, followed by the first joint meeting of the Board of Directors and Supervisors on September 4. Through the votes of these two meetings, Lin Jia-Feng was elected as the association's first President to lead its future development.

本會成立宗旨為推展特技舞蹈教育，培育表演藝術跨域人才，建構專業證照檢定，鏈結產業需求，促進產業生態發展，擴大特技舞蹈國際能量。

The association is dedicated to promoting acrobatic dance education, cultivating cross-domain performing arts talents, building professional certification systems linked to industry needs, advancing the development of the industry ecosystem, and expanding the international reach of acrobatic dance.

宗旨Mission

  • 本會為依法設立、非以營利為目的社會團體。
  • A legally established, non-profit social organization.
  • 推展特技舞蹈教育，培育表演藝術跨域人才。
  • Promoting acrobatic dance education and cultivating cross-domain performing arts talents.
  • 建構專業證照檢定，鏈結產業需求。
  • Building professional certification systems linked to industry needs.
  • 促進產業生態發展，擴大特技舞蹈國際能量為宗旨。
  • Advancing the industry ecosystem and expanding the international reach of acrobatic dance.
